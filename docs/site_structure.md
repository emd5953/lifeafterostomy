# Life After Ostomy - Site Structure & Navigation

## Main Navigation Structure

### Primary Navigation Bar
```
Home | About Us | Products | Ostomy News | Ostomy Knowledge | Ostomy Events | Contact Us | Login
```

### Secondary Navigation (User Area)
```
Dashboard | My Orders | Profile | Reorder | Logout
```

## Detailed Page Structure

### 1. Home Page (`/`)
- **Hero Section:** Brand introduction and mission
- **Featured Products:** Ostomy Care Kits highlights
- **Recent News:** Latest ostomy-related updates
- **Social Media Feed:** Live integration with all platforms
- **Call-to-Action:** Newsletter signup, product browsing

### 2. About Us (`/about`)
- **Brand Story:** Mission and vision
- **Founder Information:** Personal journey and expertise
- **Why Choose Us:** Unique value proposition
- **Community Impact:** Testimonials and success stories
- **Team Information:** Key personnel and qualifications

### 3. Products (`/products`)
- **Product Categories:**
  - Ostomy Care Kits (Complete)
  - Individual Care Items
  - Books by Age Group
  - Accessories and Supplies
- **Product Pages:** Detailed descriptions, images, reviews
- **Shopping Cart:** Add to cart functionality
- **Wishlist:** Save for later feature

### 4. Ostomy News (`/news`)
- **Latest Articles:** Current ostomy-related news
- **Medical Updates:** New treatments and research
- **Community Stories:** User experiences and tips
- **Industry News:** Product releases and innovations
- **Search and Filter:** By date, category, topic

### 5. Ostomy Knowledge (`/knowledge`)
- **Educational Articles:** Comprehensive guides
- **FAQ Section:** Common questions and answers
- **Video Library:** Educational content
- **Downloadable Resources:** PDFs, guides, checklists
- **Search Functionality:** Easy content discovery

### 6. Ostomy Events (`/events`)
- **Upcoming Events:** Conferences, workshops, webinars
- **Past Events:** Recordings and summaries
- **Community Meetups:** Local and virtual gatherings
- **Educational Workshops:** Skill-building sessions
- **Event Calendar:** Interactive scheduling

### 7. Contact Us (`/contact`)
- **Contact Form:** General inquiries
- **Support Information:** Customer service details
- **Business Hours:** Operating schedule
- **Location Information:** Physical address if applicable
- **Social Media Links:** Direct access to all platforms

### 8. User Authentication (`/login`, `/register`)
- **Login Form:** Secure user authentication
- **Registration:** New user account creation
- **Password Recovery:** Forgot password functionality
- **Profile Management:** User information updates
- **Order History:** Previous purchase tracking

## Additional Page Types

### Product Detail Pages (`/products/{slug}`)
- **Product Images:** High-quality photo gallery
- **Detailed Description:** Comprehensive product information
- **Specifications:** Technical details and dimensions
- **Customer Reviews:** User feedback and ratings
- **Related Products:** Cross-selling opportunities
- **Add to Cart:** Purchase functionality

### Blog/Article Pages (`/news/{slug}`, `/knowledge/{slug}`)
- **Article Content:** Full text with formatting
- **Author Information:** Writer credentials
- **Publication Date:** Timestamp and updates
- **Social Sharing:** Easy sharing options
- **Related Articles:** Content recommendations

### User Dashboard (`/dashboard`)
- **Order Status:** Current and past orders
- **Reorder Function:** Quick repurchase options
- **Profile Settings:** Personal information management
- **Preferences:** Communication and notification settings
- **Saved Items:** Wishlist and bookmarks
